PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE

SHETKARI UNNATI MANDALI Educational Trust had always imparted quality education for overall development of students right from nursery. SPMM COLLEGE of KALHER has made a dent and established unparalleled reputation in last …….years. SPMM College therefore provides an excellent infrastructure, sports facilities, technologically upgraded Wi-Fi campus and most importantly an excellent team of highly qualified and competent faculty for imparting quality education. I firmly believe that “Education is the best gift one can gift to others and to oneself” and so let SPMM College redefine the educational scenario and nurture future educators and entrepreneurs of tomorrow. Keep winning laurels and All The Best for your endeavors!!
